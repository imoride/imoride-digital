Common Questions

Do you guarantee results? +
We guarantee the outreach — real emails sent to real targeted contacts in your city. Conversion depends on your team following up. One new job typically covers the full campaign cost many times over.
How do you find the leads? +
We use Google Maps and business directories to identify property managers, commercial clients, and local contacts in your city who are likely to need your service. All contacts are publicly listed.
How quickly do I see results? +
Most clients begin seeing replies and enquiries within the first 1 to 2 weeks. We start sending within 24 hours of receiving payment.
What do I need to provide? +
Just your business name, the city you serve, and your contact details. We handle everything else — research, writing, sending, and tracking.
How do I pay? +
We accept bank transfer, PayPal, Zelle, and Wise. Payment is required upfront before the campaign begins. Email us at info@imoride.shop to get started.
